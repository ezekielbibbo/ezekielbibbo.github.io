Project - Calculator

The focus of this project is to study and replicate the design of a TI-89 calculator. The project began with two drawings: one illustrating the external appearance of the calculator and another detailing its possible internal components. This exploration allowed for a deeper understanding of the calculator so a precise model could be fabricated for it. A detailed foam core model was created to investigate the calculator's form and ergonomics, providing a comprehensive look at this advanced computational tool.
